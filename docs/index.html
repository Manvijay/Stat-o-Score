Welcome to the world of Stats and Scores. We present to you the new era of android sports applications.

App Features

Current Matches

Keep track of score of all the matches going on in the top 3 European Soccer Leagues, English Premier League, Bundesliga and La Liga .

Rankings

Ranking tables of top 3 European Soccer Leagues at one place. Keep track of weekly changes in the ranks of your favorite Soccer teams in the top leagues of European Soccer .

Battlefield

Compare top players in Soccer world based on their FIFA stats. Want to see the stats of less popular players? No worry! We have a database of 17,000 soccer players .

Using Instructions
Instuctions for Non-Technical Users.

1. You can download the app by clicking on the Download button present on top of this page.

2. Tap on the .apk file which you just downloaded and the file manager will automatically install the file on your device. (Note: In some cases you just have to give special permission for this app to be installed on your device. Don't Worry this app won't harm your mobile/tablet, It's just that mobile devices by default don't allow any files from unknown sources.)

3. Cheers! The app is installed on your device.

4. After you open the app you will see 3 Horizontally scrollable scorecards of English Premiere League, La Liga and BundesLiga. Swipe left/margin-right to keep track of scores.

5. You can also see a black bar(navigation bar) on the bottom of your screen. You can use that to hop between different sections of this app.

6. Tap on Rankings to see the latest ranking tables of the aforementioned soccer leagues.

7. In the Battlefield, you can just type the name of two players you want to compare and then based on FIFA acknowledged stats our app will declare a winner out of the two.

8. If these instructions weren't very useful to you then you can go to our YouTube channel and watch the videos of Stats-o-Score.

9. Enjoy using Stats-o-Score!

Instructions for Technical Users (Testers)

For Developer to run the you need to download and setup android studio. If you don’t have android studio setup already please refer to this link to setup android studio https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=F-k5gwz_91o (Watch till 15 min only) After that follow these to run our app in android studio.

1. First got to our github repo and copy the our clone https link from here .

2. Now open android studio and choose Checkout Version Control -> GitHub (If you don’t see this window click on File -> Close Project).

3. Now you have to paste the Github clone url we copied earlier here. Select your parent Directory and/or a name for Directory Name.

4. Click and clone let the process go on.

5. After cloning is done, you might get an error. Don’t worry just click on .

6. Android Studio will start working on setting up your project.

7. After the process is done(don’t worry if it’s still giving an error) click on Build -> Clean Project

8. After its done click on Build -> Build Apk(s).

9. After that click on Sync Project with Gradle Files icon. If you can’t find the icon just click Tools -> Android -> Sync Gradle Files.

10. Finally click on Run to start the project.

11. Select your own AVD device. Enjoy running the app!

Help us making our app better. Go to our GitHub issue page and report the issue/advice.

About the App

Our goal was to make an Android App which will add a new dimension to the world of Android Sports Applications. This sports app named "Stats-o-Score" will provide a new vision to the sports app category. The name of this app is the combination of "Stats" and "Scores" which is what this app provides to it's users, alongwith one of its kind feature of allowing users to compare two players based on their stats.
Usually sports app tick few important boxes, like providing stats for players or provide scores of matches. However, we plan on adding a new dimesnion to that and add sort of a minigame within the app, which is like a battle arena. Here, the user can compare two players and see which one comes on top. As of now this app is focussing on soccer and bringing to you live scores and rankings of your favorite teams playing in the top European leagues but we plan on adding more sports down the road.
